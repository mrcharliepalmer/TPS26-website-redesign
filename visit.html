The Business Design Centre

One of London's most iconic event spaces, the BDC sits in the heart of Islington — one of the capital's most vibrant neighbourhoods. With excellent transport links, world-class facilities, and an atmosphere built for networking, it's the perfect home for the world's biggest podcasting festival.

Business Design Centre
52 Upper Street
Islington, London N1 0QH

Key Information

Dates

Wednesday 20 & Thursday 21 May 2026

Hours

Registration from 8:30am. Doors open 9am. Show closes 6pm both days.

Registration

Collect your badge at the Entrance Hall. Bring your booking confirmation (digital or printed) and photo ID.

Night Programme

Evening events run separately. Check your pass tier or purchase evening tickets on site.

Getting There

The BDC is one of the best-connected venues in London. Central, walkable, and easy to reach from all major stations and airports.

By Tube

Angel Station

Northern Line — 5 minute walk. Exit the station, turn left onto Upper Street, and the BDC is on your right.

By Rail

Highbury & Islington

Overground, Victoria Line, and National Rail services. A 10 minute walk south along Upper Street.

By Bus

Upper Street Stops

Routes 4, 19, 30, 43, and 271 all stop along Upper Street within walking distance of the venue.

By Car

Limited Parking

The BDC has no on-site car park. Nearby NCP car parks include Islington and Finsbury Square. We recommend public transport.

By Air

London Airports

City Airport: 30 mins. Heathrow: 60 mins via Piccadilly Line. Gatwick: 60 mins via Thameslink to King's Cross.

By Bike

Cycle Hire

Santander Cycles docking stations on Upper Street and surrounding roads. Bike racks available at the BDC entrance.

Where to Stay

Islington has plenty of accommodation options within walking distance of the BDC. Book early — rooms fill up fast around the festival.

The Standard, London

King's Cross. A 15-minute walk or short bus ride. Host venue for The Official Party.

Hilton Islington

Upper Street. Directly opposite the BDC — the closest hotel to the venue.

Premier Inn Angel

Liverpool Road. Budget-friendly, 5 minutes' walk from the venue.

Airbnb & Aparthotels

Islington and Clerkenwell offer great flat rentals. Ideal for groups or longer stays.

Accessibility

The Podcast Show is committed to being accessible to everyone. The Business Design Centre is a fully accessible venue with step-free access throughout.

  • Step-free access to all levels and stages
  • Accessible toilets on all floors
  • Induction hearing loops in main theatres
  • Dedicated quiet spaces available
  • Service animals welcome
  • Complimentary carer/PA passes

If you have specific accessibility requirements, please contact team@thepodcastshowlondon.com and we'll make arrangements in advance.

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring?

Your booking confirmation (digital is fine), photo ID, and business cards if you have them. Cloakroom facilities are available at the venue.

Is there food and drink at the venue?

Yes. The BDC has several catering outlets including The Hub Bar, plus there are hundreds of restaurants and cafes along Upper Street within a 2-minute walk.

Can I attend just one day?

Yes. Gold 1-Day passes give you full access for either Wednesday or Thursday. You choose your day at booking.

Is there Wi-Fi at the venue?

Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the Business Design Centre for all attendees.

What are the evening events?

The night programme includes The Creator Mix (Day 1 evening), The Official Party, live podcasts at iconic London venues, and partner receptions. Evening tickets are separate from daytime passes — check your pass tier for what's included.

Is there a cloakroom?

Yes. A staffed cloakroom is available in the Entrance Hall for bags and coats.
