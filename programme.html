The Big Themes

The programme is shaped by the conversations driving the industry right now.

The AI Transformation

How artificial intelligence is reshaping production, discovery, and monetisation — and what it means for your workflow.

Multi-Platform Strategy

YouTube, TikTok, newsletters, live events. How the smartest podcasters are building audiences beyond the feed.

Evolving Revenue Models

Beyond host-read ads. Subscriptions, programmatic, licensing, live, merch — the new revenue stack.

Community & Fandom

Building audiences that stick. Superfans, membership models, and turning listeners into communities.

The Global Opportunity

Podcasting is booming worldwide. International markets, cross-border deals, and what the UK can learn.

Streaming's Podcast Push

Spotify, Amazon, YouTube — how the platforms are reshaping the landscape and what it means for independents.

True Crime's Evolution

The genre that built podcasting is maturing. New formats, ethical conversations, and what's next.

Branded Content & Advertising

The future of podcast advertising. Data, measurement, branded shows, and the agency perspective.

Podcasting as Social Media

Clips, short-form, comments, engagement. How podcasting is becoming a social-first medium.
